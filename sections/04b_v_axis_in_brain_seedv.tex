\section{Replication on a Second EEG Dataset (SEED-V)}
\label{sec:vaxis-brain-seedv}

We re-test all five FACED claims on SEED-V \citep{liu2022seedv} (16
subjects, 5 classes, 62 channels) with a V-axis re-built from scratch
on the 5 SEED-V classes. All five re-emerge: cohort EEG--LLM
$r{=}+0.6159$ at P1/$\theta$ ($p<10^{-5}$); posterior dominance
$+0.076$; cross-arch $r{=}+0.601$ ($p=0.018$, $N{=}15$); supervision
delta within seed noise ($\Delta=+0.0025$, n.s.); and causal closure ---
Arditi-style ablation drops BACC on every one of the 15 SEED-V
checkpoints, $\Delta\mathrm{BACC} \in [-0.029, -0.009]$ at mean $z=+23.6$
above a matched random-direction null (all $p<0.001$), proportionally
larger than FACED's effect. Full Step 1--5 numerics, FAA bars, and
per-checkpoint details in Appendix~\ref{app:seedv-full}.
