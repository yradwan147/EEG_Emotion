\textbf{Stimulus-level alignment between the LLM valence axis and human EEG.}
Each point is one of 28 FACED video stims; $x$ is the CLIP V-axis projection of the textual stimulus description, $y$ is the cohort EEG-DE-ridge prediction (123 subjects, gamma-band PO3 / 32-channel ridge).
Pearson $r{=}0.874$, $p<10^{-9}$.
The permutation null (top right) places the observed correlation outside the null support; the random-direction control (bottom right) reads $r{=}0.07$.
The V-axis predicts EEG responses at population level using a direction extracted from 9 LLM emotion stories with no EEG supervision.
